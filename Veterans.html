Who We Are

San Bernardino County Veterans Affairs is dedicated to serving veterans, their families, and survivors by providing free services to access benefits, and resources they have earned.

What We Do

We can help you apply for:

  • Disability & Pension benefits
  • Education & Vocational Readiness & Employment Programs
  • California College Fee Waivers
  • VA Health Care
  • Burial & Memorial Benefits
