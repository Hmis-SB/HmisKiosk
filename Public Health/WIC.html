Women, Infants and Children Program

Welcome to the San Bernardino County Department of Public Health WIC Program

For more than 35 years, the San Bernardino Department of Public Health WIC program has helped expectant families, new parents, infants, and young children get healthy foods, nutrition education, breastfeeding support, and community resources.

This program is funded by the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA).
